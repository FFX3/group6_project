Where to Eat in Canada

This website was created by group 6 in York University's Fall 2020 Full Stack Web Development Certificate Program. It was designed to help the user find a place to eat depending on the province they live in.

How It Works

Simply click on a province and be redirected to a list of the most mouth watering restuaurants your province has to offer!

Feel free to send us message if you know a restuarant deserving of having a spot on our website.
